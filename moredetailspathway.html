NON-PROFIT RESPONSIVE REDESIGN

RESEARCH PROCESS

Civic Helper is a mobile app that makes it easy to discover, schedule and manage volunteer opportunities.

Civic Helper is a travel app that took a few weeks in the design process. My team wanted to come up with a useful tool to generate more civic engagement. Some creative brainstorming on a Miro board helped us gather some inspiration. There was a drive to design a useful tool that would add value while letting users discover, schedule and manage volunteer opportunities.

The goal in the research phase was to gain insight into what types of civic engagement users are interested in and their current frustrations with getting involved. We conducted interviews over Zoom. The participants were between the ages of 28-40 who are currently interested in volunteering.

.

.

IDEATION PROCESS

The hurdles users mostly run into, is not knowing how or where to start for civic engagement. It can be difficult finding volunteer opportunities. Civic Helper is a tool that can be used with its main feature utilizing a simple calendar view! The app will make it easy to find, manage, schedule all volunteer opportunities at your fingertips! We’ve added features that narrow down your searches by location and by date.

TESTING PROCESS

User testing sessions were very helpful with providing feedback. This created a list of improvements that could be made. Some of these were the build out of the hamburger menu, and the increase of the font size. Iterations were made to the calendar as well.

PROTOTYPE & OPPORTUNITIES

In order to get an app that took overall less time for a user to join a volunteer opportunity we needed to add more working visual queues and flexibility to the app. We did this by getting more out of Adobe XD with the features it has so we could have greater usability within fewer sections in the app

Some great next steps would be to conduct conduct trial runs with local non-profits as a way to introduce the tool to potential users. Once the Civic Helper is fully adopted by users, we believe it will increase the number of times a typical user volunteers each month. Users will be able to use the time they’d normally spend looking for opportunities, actively volunteering.
